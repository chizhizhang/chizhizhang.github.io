Biography

Chizhi Chris Zhang is an Associate Professor at the Changchun Institute of Optics, Fine Mechanics and Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and a PhD Supervisor in Signal Processing at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

"Numbers rule the universe."

Pythagoras

His work connects AI, signal processing, and reliability analysis for engineering systems, with current interests in diagnosis, prognosis, and health assessment under uncertainty.

Research Areas
Trustworthy AI for Engineering Systems Signal-based Diagnosis Reliability and PHM Predictive Modelling under Uncertainty
Student Opportunities

PhD and master's applicants through UCAS are welcome. Students with backgrounds in signal processing, applied mathematics, mechanics, control, computing, or data analysis are especially encouraged to apply.
