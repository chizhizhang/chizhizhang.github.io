Course Overview

One course linking intelligent systems and learning methods.

The course introduces major concepts in artificial intelligence together with foundational neural-network methods used in modern prediction and decision systems.

Course Structure

Two parts within the same course.

  • Part I. Artificial Intelligence: intelligent agents, search, representation, and reasoning.
  • Part II. Neural Networks: neural models, training ideas, and data-driven prediction methods.
